RWPI Logic Guide

The Risk-Weighted Productivity Index (RWPI) evaluates each individual ticket processed by an employee based on its intrinsic categorical effort, business priority, and relative impact on the department's total backlog.

β_i Base Effort Points

An objective, fixed point value assigned automatically by the ticketing system based on the category (e.g., Routine=10, Involved=20, Complex=40).

λ_i Priority Multiplier

Scales the reward geometrically based on the ticket's predefined criticality (Minor to Critical).

Impact Backlog Bonus

Mathematically rewards an analyst for solving the desk's actual debt. If a ticket clears 20% of the desk's total backlog, the analyst gets a 1.20x bonus. The max(N, n, 1) denominator acts as a safeguard to prevent system crashes (division by zero) and nullifies "ghost data" exploits.

α_i SLA Aging Multiplier

An automated governance switch. Tickets resolved on time receive a 1.0x multiplier. Tickets that breach their SLA incur a 10% penalty (0.9x), enforcing urgency and protecting against audit breaches.
